Established In The Wilderness

Your Gateway to Corbett

Located in the serene Dhela Village and sharing a boundary with the renowned Corbett Tiger Reserve, Corbett Treat Resort offers 24 well-appointed rooms designed for pure comfort. Our resort provides an ideal escape for nature lovers, offering stunning opportunities for wildlife sightings and birdwatching right from the property. Wake up to the calls of the wild and immerse yourself in absolute tranquility.
